Protector Of The 49th Parallel is performance for camera where I used Canada's national sport, Ice hockey, to express issues surrounding the new environmental policy changes. As a goalie I made it my goal to stop as many fish as possible from being shot into the net.

I use the humour of this unlikely task as a way to bring attention to the discussions/politics around the recent closures of numerous Canadian research fisheries and libraries.

The second work of the series, Puck You, compliments the first by packaging hockey pucks within tin cans normally used for fish.
