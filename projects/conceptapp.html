Concept App Design

  • July 2013
  • Design

The purpose of this concept application that I created is similar to any sports analytic app. However instead of regular sports, it follows an Esports game called League of Legends.

This was a project that I worked on in college and interestingly enough within a few months after creating it I saw similar versions published on the apple app store. It was entirely done in Adobe Photoshop and Flash while pulling content from Riot games' API and site.
